Since September 2019, I am a post-doc researcher in LaMME at the University of Paris-Saclay. Before this, I was a Ph.D student in Institute Camille Jordan at the University Lyon 1 under the supervise of Dragoş Iftimie and Lorenzo Brandolese.

I have defendes my Ph.D thesis in September 2019.

I work mainly on the analysis of partial differential equations. My research focuses on the well-posedness, global behavior of models arising from Navier-Stokes equations. I am also interested in related problems on the dynamics of electrified falling films, for example, the Kuramoto-Sivashinsky-type equations.
