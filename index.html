I am currently a Post-doc researcher in LaMME at the University of Paris-Saclay. My post-doc mentor is Diego Chamorro.
I finished my Ph.D in Institute Camille Jordan at the University Bernard Claude Lyon 1 under the supervise of Dragoş Iftimie and Lorenzo Brandolese.
I defended my Ph.D thesis on 20 September 2019 and my dissertation is available Here.

I work mainly on the analysis of partial differential equations. My research focuses on the well-posedness, regularity theory and global behavior of models arising from Navier-Stokes equations. I am also interested in the water-wave equations ‍and ‍related dispersive ‍models.
